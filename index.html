Privacy Policy Fungus Stats Bot

Last updated: September 29, 2025

1) Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how Fungus Stats Bot ("the Bot") processes information when you use it on Discord. By using the Bot, you agree to this Policy.

2) Information We Process

The Bot does not request names, emails, or passwords. It may temporarily process:

  • Discord IDs: user, server (guild), channel, and message IDs—to respond to commands.
  • Command Content: parameters you provide (e.g., amounts, farm names) to compute outputs.
  • Usage Data: counts of commands, errors, and runtime diagnostics to improve stability.

We do not sell, rent, or trade any data with third parties.

3) How We Use Information

  • Provide features like calculators and stats.
  • Fix bugs, improve performance, and prevent spam/abuse.
  • Ensure fair use and service reliability.

4) Data Storage & Retention

  • Most data is handled in-memory and not stored long-term.
  • Optional logs (if enabled) are kept for debugging and rotated/cleared regularly.
  • No cross‑service tracking or profiling.

5) Legal Basis

We process limited data to provide the service you request (legitimate interest / performance of a service).

6) Your Rights & Controls

  • You can stop using the Bot at any time.
  • To request deletion of log entries containing your user ID, contact the owner listed below with your Discord tag and ID.

7) Third‑Party Services

The Bot uses the Discord API. Your use of Discord is also governed by Discord’s Privacy Policy and Terms.

8) Security

We use reasonable measures to secure systems and limit access to logs. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

9) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot after changes means you accept the updated version.

10) Contact
